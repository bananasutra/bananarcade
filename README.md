# 🍌 bananarcade

**A little arcade for the end of the guardrails.** Six ways into **3,466 documented actions** (Jan 20, 2025 → Jun 26, 2026) from [trumpactiontracker.info](https://trumpactiontracker.info), across ten categories of democratic erosion: four games, an evidence room, and a model.

You play the guardrails. Mostly you lose — the only variable is how fast. (One game, you can actually win.)

🎮 **Play it:** open `index.html` in any browser — no install, no backend, no tracking. Or visit the live page below.

---

## The six

1. **Overwhelm** *(game)* — You are the justice system. Actions fall at the real, accelerating rate; you can only process one at a time. The backlog is the point.
2. **Guardrail Collapse** *(game)* — Eight institutions, each drained monthly by the real category counts. Reinforce one per month. Watch the rest fall.
3. **Sort the Flood** *(game)* — Triage real logged actions into the right categories before a shrinking clock empties. Speed plus complexity breaks oversight.
4. **Hold Back Midnight** *(game — winnable)* — Turn-based: each quarter the documented structural pressure pushes the clock toward midnight. Spend limited civic capacity across courts, press, protest, and ballots — and bank ballots for the 2028 election quarters. Survive to Nov 2028 above midnight and you win. Casual play loses; good play wins by a hair.
5. **The Record + Forecast** *(evidence room)* — The documented velocity, the category breakdown, the doomsday clock (with its math shown in plain language), and two clearly-labeled trend extrapolations to Nov 2028.
6. **Scenario Model** *(interactive tool)* — Two sliders — how fast actions grow, how hard institutions push back — drive a live projection to 2028. A sandbox for assumptions, explicitly not a forecast.

A **Satire / Sober** voice toggle (top-right) rewrites every screen's copy so you can choose the tone.

## Honest by design

The action counts, dates, and category flags are **documented and arithmetic — not opinion.** The 2028 projections are **simple extrapolations, clearly labeled as such** — a trajectory ("if nothing changes"), not a forecast of inevitability. Courts, elections, and public pressure are not in this dataset and can change the line.

**The numbers are real. The dread is existential.**
