# 🍌 bananarcade

**A little arcade for the end of the guardrails.** Four small browser games built on **3,466 documented actions** (Jan 20, 2025 → Jun 26, 2026) from [trumpactiontracker.info](https://trumpactiontracker.info), across ten categories of democratic erosion.

You play the guardrails. You lose. The only variable is how fast.

🎮 **Play it:** open `index.html` in any browser — no install, no backend, no tracking. Or visit the live page below.

---

## The games

- **Overwhelm** — You are the justice system. Actions fall at the real, accelerating rate; you can only process one at a time. The backlog is the point.
- **Guardrail Collapse** — Eight institutions, each drained monthly by the real category counts. Reinforce one per month. Watch the rest fall.
- **Sort the Flood** — Triage real logged actions into the right categories before a shrinking clock empties. Speed plus complexity breaks oversight.
- **Countdown to 2028** — A doomsday-clock dashboard: documented data in one color, two clearly-labeled trend extrapolations to the Nov 2028 election in another.

A **Satire / Sober** voice toggle (top-right) rewrites every game's copy so you can choose the tone.

## Honest by design

The action counts, dates, and category flags are **documented and arithmetic — not opinion.** The 2028 projections are **simple extrapolations, clearly labeled as such** — a trajectory ("if nothing changes"), not a forecast of inevitability. Courts, elections, and public pressure are not in this dataset and can change the line.

**The numbers are real. The dread is editorial.**
