What is data and what is projection? (read this)
Documented (solid, on the record): every action count, date, and category flag comes directly from the trumpactiontracker.info dataset of actions. The velocity curve and category totals are arithmetic — not opinion.

Projection (dashed): the two 2028 lines are simple extrapolations — one holds the recent monthly average flat, one re-applies the steeper 2025 growth rate. They illustrate a trajectory, not a prediction. Real institutions can reverse course; courts, elections, and public pressure are not in this dataset. Treat the dashed lines as "if nothing changes," not "what will happen."

The honest claim: the documented record shows a sustained, high-volume pace of actions concentrated in rule-of-law, dissent-suppression, and press categories. Whether that culminates in the scenario this arcade dramatizes is a question for citizens, courts, and voters — not a result the spreadsheet can deliver.
